Vidyut Dev Singh Jammwal

Vidyut Dev Singh Jammwal (born 10 December 1980)[1][2] is an Indian actor, martial artist[3] and film producer who predominantly works in Hindi, Tamil and Telugu films. He is also a practitioner of Kalaripayattu, which he showcased in the film Junglee (2019).[4][5] He is best known for his roles in the Commando film series, and is a recipient of several accolades, including a Stardust Award, FICCI Frames Award, Filmfare Award, and IIFA Award.[6]
